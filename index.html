ABOUT ME

Painter / Photographer / Activist

I grew up in Bologna, where I had the opportunity to receive my cultural influences. There, I studied the High school of Art and the I got the Degree in cinema and Visual arts. I developed my sensibility when I started to explore the "Planet" around me. Because, After studies, I started "the trip of my life" that brought me to live to Tenerife, Vigo, London, Luxembourg and, nowadays, Valencia.

I am multidisciplinary artist, I work with paintings and photography to try to transmit my vision of the contemporary World. The social critics of the system is the base in which I develop my artworks. I like to consider myself an artist activist, because with the artistic commitment I add a social commitment. Especially towards human rights and the social discrimination of minorities who have an out-of-the-box thinking. My artworks are the product of my meditations. My art is my way to try to change the World !
